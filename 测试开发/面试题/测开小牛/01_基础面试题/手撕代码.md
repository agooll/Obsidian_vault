### 17. 寻找旋转排序数组中的最小值

```text
publicintfindMin(int[] nums){int left =0, right = nums.length -1;while(left < right){int mid = left +(right - left)/2;if(nums[mid]> nums[right]){            left = mid +1;}else{            right = mid;}}return nums[left];}// 测试用例// System.out.println(findMin(new int[]{3, 4, 5, 1, 2})); // 1
```
